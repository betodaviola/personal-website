About

Roberto Mochetti is a viola player, teacher, arranger, and composer. Passionate about experimental and chamber music, he has played, studied, and taught in Brazil, the United States, and in Denmark. Today, Roberto is working on his Doctor of Musical Arts degree at LSU, focusing on viola performance with a minor in experimental music and digital media. For more information, visit the bio page.
